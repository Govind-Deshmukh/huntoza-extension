Get Started with PursuitPal

PursuitPal helps you organize your job search by automatically extracting details from job postings on popular sites like LinkedIn, Indeed, and more!

Track Applications

Save job details with a single click when browsing job sites. All your applications in one place!

Save Time

No more manual data entry - we'll extract the company, position, job description, and more automatically.

Getting Started

  1. Sign up for a PursuitPal account using the login page (or log in if you already have one)
  2. Browse job sites like LinkedIn, Indeed, or Glassdoor as normal
  3. Click the PursuitPal icon when you're on a job posting you're interested in
  4. Review the extracted details and click "Send to Job Tracker"

Step 1: Sign in to get started

To use PursuitPal, you'll need to sign in first. Click the button below to open the login page.

How to Use PursuitPal

1. Visit job boards

Visit job boards like LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, and find job postings you're interested in.

PursuitPal works with: LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, ZipRecruiter, Monster, Lever.co, Greenhouse.io, and many more!

2. Click on the PursuitPal icon

Click on the PursuitPal icon in your browser extensions bar. The sidebar will appear with job details automatically extracted.

If the job details aren't extracted automatically, you can click the "Refresh" button to try again.

3. Review and send to tracker

Review the extracted job details, make any necessary adjustments, and click "Send to Job Tracker" to save it to your account.

4. Manage your applications

Visit the PursuitPal web app to manage your tracked applications, add notes, set reminders, and track your progress.
