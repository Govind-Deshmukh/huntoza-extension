About PursuitPal Extension

PursuitPal is a Chrome extension that helps you track your job hunt by easily extracting job details and LinkedIn contacts.

All data is stored locally on your browser. Your credentials and extracted data never leave your browser except when you explicitly click "Send to PursuitPal".

How to use:

  1. Visit a job posting page on LinkedIn, Indeed, Naukri, or other job sites
  2. Click the PursuitPal extension icon to extract job details
  3. Send the extracted data to your PursuitPal account
  4. Use the same process for LinkedIn profiles to extract contact details
