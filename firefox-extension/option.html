About PursuitPal

PursuitPal is a job hunting assistant that helps you track and organize your job search. This extension integrates with your PursuitPal account to automatically extract job details from popular job boards.

Your data is always synced with your PursuitPal account. Login using the same credentials as the PursuitPal web app.

How to use:

  1. Visit a job posting page (LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, etc.)
  2. Click the PursuitPal extension icon in your browser
  3. The extension will automatically extract job details
  4. Click "Save to PursuitPal" to add the job to your account
