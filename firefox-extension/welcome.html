Getting Started

  1. 1

    Sign in to your PursuitPal account

    Click the PursuitPal extension icon and sign in using your existing account credentials.

  2. 2

    Visit a job posting page

    Browse job boards like LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, Naukri or others. The extension icon will show a "JOB" badge when a job posting is detected.

  3. 3

    Extract and review job details

    Click the extension icon to automatically extract job details. Review and edit them if needed.

  4. 4

    Save to your account

    Click "Save to PursuitPal" to add the job to your account. You'll be redirected to the PursuitPal web app.

Tips and Tricks

  • Editable fields - All fields in the preview are editable. Click on any field to modify it before saving.

  • Auto-extraction - Enable auto-extraction in the extension settings to automatically extract job details when visiting a job page.

  • Default settings - Configure your default currency, priority, and other preferences in the extension settings.

Customize Your Settings

You can customize the extension behavior to match your preferences:

  1. 1. Click the PursuitPal extension icon in your browser toolbar
  2. 2. Click the "Settings" link at the bottom of the popup
  3. 3. Adjust your preferences for auto-extraction, notifications, default values, and more
  4. 4. Click "Save Settings" to apply your changes
